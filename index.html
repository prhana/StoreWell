Patient Record Retention 
Massage therapists must keep a record of client evaluations and treatments for six years or until the client turns 22, whichever is longer. Clients have the right to their records and to have a copy of the records. It is the massage therapist’s responsibility to provide the client with these records. Massage therapists must get client consent, or the consent of an authorized person, before releasing personal records, except in circumstances provided for by the law. http://www.op.nysed.gov/prof/mt/mtbroch.htm
